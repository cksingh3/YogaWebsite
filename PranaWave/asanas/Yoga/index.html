Yoga Poses for Every Part of Your Body

One of the beauties of yoga is that many poses work your whole body. Unlike having a "legs day" or "arms day" at the gym, doing a regular yoga practice can easily incorporate your entire anatomy. Even if you pick a body part you want to work on and tailor a practice that way, you'll still feel the benefits elsewhere. This guide will help you find poses that strengthen and/or open major muscle groups. For each anatomical area, you'll see a list of poses divided into beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels of practice. Use these to plan your yoga sessions or find out more about the poses you do in class. Following these are a few pre-planned sequences that target that area of the body.
